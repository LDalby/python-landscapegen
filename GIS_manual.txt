Preliminary guide - steps to prepare Norwegian maps for the landscape generator
January 20 - 2016

Input: corrected Matrikkel-kort  ("matrikkel")

Step 1 - Combine "matrikkel" and "ar5_flate" and add matrikkel information to all polygons
Tool: Identify
Input features: "ar5_flate"
Identify features: "matrikkel"
Output features: "ar5_flate_identify"

Step 2 -  Check geometry
Tool: Repair geometry
Input featurs: "ar5_flate_identify"

Step 3 - Calculate area
Open attribute table for "ar5_flate_identify"
Right-click column "AREAL" and calculate geometry. Choose 'Area' in hectares

Step 4 - Convert multipart polygons to single polygons
Tool: Multipart to singlepart
Input "ar5_flate_identify"
Output "ar5_flate_identify_single"

Step 5 - Remove sliver polygons
First select all polygons <= 0.01 hectares
Tool: Eliminate
Input: "ar5_flate_identify_single"
Output: "ar5_flate_final"
